🏗️ The ConceptBridge House Coffee isn't just a cafe; it’s a destination. Located at the intersection of quality craft and local comfort, we bridge the gap between high-end specialty coffee and a "home away from home" atmosphere.Our Core Pillars:Sourcing: Direct-trade beans from sustainable micro-lots.Craft: Precision brewing (V60, Chemex, and calibrated Espresso).Community: A space designed for lingering, conversation, and collaboration.📋 Quick NavigationSectionDescriptionMenu StandardsCurrent seasonal offerings and signature lattes.Brand AssetsLogos, typography (serif/sans-serif mix), and color palette.Staff ManualOpening/closing procedures and dial-in guides.Supplier ListContact info for roasters and local dairies.

🎨 Brand IdentityPrimary Palette: Deep Navy, Toasted Almond, and Slate Grey.Voice: Knowledgeable but never pretentious; warm and inviting.Signature Element: The "Bridge" icon—symbolizing the connection between the farmer and the guest.

🚀 Getting Started (For New Staff/Partners)The Dial-In: Every morning starts with a double-shot extraction test ($18g$ in, $36g$ out in $27-30$ seconds).The Greeting: Every guest is a neighbor. Acknowledge everyone within 10 seconds of entry.The Environment: Keep the "Bridge" clean—the counter is our stage.
