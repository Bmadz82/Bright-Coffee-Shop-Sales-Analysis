 ☕ Bright Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing transactional sales data from a fictional coffee shop, **Bright Coffee Shop**, to uncover actionable business insights. The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful information that supports data-driven decision-making.

The dataset captures day-to-day sales activities, including product performance, store performance, customer purchasing behavior, and time-based trends.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-performing products and categories
Understand customer purchasing patterns
Determine peak sales periods (days and times)
Evaluate store performance across locations
Provide actionable recommendations to improve revenue

---

📁 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of transactional records typically found in retail coffee shop environments. It includes fields such as:

Transaction_id** – Unique identifier for each transaction
Transaction_date & transaction_time** – Date and time of purchase
store_id & store_location** – Store information
product_id, product_category, product_type, product_detail** – Product details
transaction_qty** – Quantity purchased
unit_price** – Price per item
Revenue** – Total transaction value
day_name, month_name, Time_classification** – Derived time attributes

Such datasets are commonly used to analyze **sales trends, customer behavior, and operational performance** in coffee shop environments.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

SQL** – Data cleaning and transformation
Excel – Data visualization and dashboard creation



📈 Key Analysis Areas

 1. Sales Performance
 2. Total Revenue
 3. Total Transactions
 4. Total Quantity Sold

2. Product Analysis

* Best-selling products
* Revenue by product category
* Product demand patterns

3. Time-Based Analysis

* Sales by day of the week
* Monthly performance trends
* Peak hours (morning, afternoon, evening)

4. Store Performance

* Revenue by store location
* Comparison of store performance

5. Customer Behavior Insights

* Purchase frequency
* Spending patterns
* Product preferences


 💡 Key Insights (Example)

* Peak sales occur during **morning hours**
* Certain product categories contribute the most to revenue
* Specific stores outperform others consistently
* Sales trends vary significantly across months and days

---

🚀 Business Recommendations

* Optimize staffing during peak hours
* Promote high-performing products
* Improve underperforming store locations
* Introduce targeted promotions during slow periods

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to turn raw transactional data into valuable business insights. By leveraging tools like Power BI and SQL, businesses can better understand performance, improve operations, and increase profitability.
